Group Classes

Group classes at Vita Gym offer an energetic and motivating environment to push yourself to new limits. Whether you're new to fitness or an experienced athlete, our diverse range of classes are designed to help you achieve your fitness goals in a fun and supportive atmosphere. From high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to yoga, there is something for everyone!

Our Group Class Offerings

Explore a variety of group classes designed to suit all fitness levels. From high-energy workouts to mindful practices, we have something for everyone.

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Get your heart pumping with our HIIT classes, designed to burn fat and build strength in a short period of time. Challenge yourself with fast-paced, full-body workouts that will leave you feeling accomplished.

Yoga

Find your inner peace and flexibility in our yoga classes. Suitable for all levels, our instructors guide you through different poses, helping you relax, stretch, and strengthen your body and mind.

Cycling

Hop on a bike and join our cycling classes for an intense, calorie-burning workout. Our indoor cycling sessions are high-energy and led by instructors who keep you motivated throughout.
